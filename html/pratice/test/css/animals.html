Animals

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is a powerful carnivore with a loud roar.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They have long trunks and big ears.

Tiger

Tigers have beautiful orange fur with black stripes. They are strong hunters and live mostly in forests.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest animals in the world. They use their long necks to eat leaves from tall trees.

Kangaroo

Kangaroos are found in Australia and have strong hind legs. They carry their babies in a pouch.

Pandas

Pandas are gentle animals that eat mostly bamboo. They have black and white fur and live in China.

Dolphine

Dolphins are smart and playful sea animals. They communicate using clicks and whistles.

Zebra

Zebras have black and white stripes all over their bodies. They are fast runners and live in groups.
